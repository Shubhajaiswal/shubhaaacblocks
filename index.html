AAC Blocks

PREMIUM QUALITY AAC BLOCKS FOR YOUR CONSTRUCTION NEEDS IN BAREILLY

Autoclaved Aerated Concrete (AAC) blocks are lightweight and durable building products. First conceptualized and invented by Swedish arch itect and inventor Johan Axel Eriksson in 1924, AAC blocks are particularly known for their fire, heat, and sound-resistant properties. Air being the major constituent (almost 80-85% by volume), these building products are typically lightweight, weighing only a third of the more common red clay bricks.
